Aggressive Retsuko

Synopsis

Retsuko is a mild-mannered office worker who deals with the stress of everyday adult life, annoying coworkers, and workplace politics. After bottling up her frustrations, she secretly vents her anger by singing aggressive death metal karaoke, revealing a side of herself no one expects.

Quick Verdict

A fun and relatable comedy about adult life, work stress, and finding ways to cope.

What It Feels Like

Comedy, slice-of-life, relatable, quirky, lighthearted

Why You Might Like It

  • Very relatable workplace humor
  • Short, easy-to-watch episodes
  • Likeable and expressive characters
  • Good balance of comedy and heart

Why You Might Not

  • Episodic structure may feel repetitive
  • Humor style may not click with everyone
  • More focused on daily life than big story arcs

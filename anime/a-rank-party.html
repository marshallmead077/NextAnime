I Left My A-Rank Party to Help My Former Students Reach the Dungeon Depths!

Synopsis

A skilled adventurer leaves his high-ranking party and reunites with his former students, forming a new team to explore dangerous dungeons and take on challenging quests. The series focuses on rebuilding bonds, mentoring younger adventurers, and climbing toward greater depths together.

Quick Verdict

A fun fantasy adventure with a solid mentor-and-students concept, but held back by heavy harem elements.

What It Feels Like

Fantasy, adventure, lighthearted, occasionally romantic, party-based

Why You Might Like It

  • Likeable main character
  • Party-based dungeon adventure
  • Mentor guiding former students
  • Easy-to-watch fantasy tone

Why You Might Not

  • Strong harem focus
  • Clear main girl but still becomes a multi-romance setup
  • If you dislike harem endings, this may be frustrating

86 Eighty-Six

Synopsis

The Republic of San Magnolia claims it fights its war using unmanned drones, but in reality, a persecuted group known as the Eighty-Six is forced to pilot these machines on the front lines. The story follows an idealistic officer who communicates with these soldiers from afar, and the battle-hardened squad leader who fights alongside his team in constant danger. As their paths cross, they begin to challenge the lies of their nation and the cruelty of the system they live under.

Quick Verdict

A strong, emotionally heavy sci-fi anime with serious themes and great character moments.

What It Feels Like

Dark, emotional, dramatic, intense, atmospheric

Why You Might Like It

  • Strong character development
  • Emotional storytelling
  • Powerful soundtrack and atmosphere
  • Consistent serious tone

Why You Might Not

  • Heavy themes throughout
  • Not a light or casual watch
  • Slower pacing at times
